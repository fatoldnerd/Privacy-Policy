Data Deletion Policy

This application does not collect or store personal data. If you have connected to the app via Facebook and wish to remove the app’s access to your data, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook account settings.
  2. Select Apps and Websites.
  3. Find this application in the list.
  4. Click Remove to delete the app’s permissions.

If you need further assistance, please contact us at: brad@towersfamily.net
